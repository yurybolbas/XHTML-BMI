Fare Rules Details

City Pair (Fare Basis)

LAX-SFO (XYZ123)

Changes

Charge usd 100.00 for reissue/revalidation

Note

  1. Voluntary rerouting is permitted provided the new Itinerary meets all the provisions of the new fare purchased i.E. Advance reservation/ tkt. purchase min/tax stay requirements
    1. The new itinerary is of equal or higher value. Note - at the psgrs request the full value of an existing tkt may be applied to an itinerary of lower value however no refund will apply. The difference in fare will be returned to the passenger in the form of a non-refundable travel voucher which may be applied towards the purchase of a ticket valid for transportation via aa only
    2. These fares may not be upgraded to any other fare type.
    3. Tkts at these fares may not be revalidated. Passengers holding confirmed reservations and tickets may standby for earlier/later same day aa nonstop and/or connecting flights between the same origin and destination airports. There is no additional cost provided the new flight/s and routing are applicable for the fare charged. If the change is not applicable for the fare charged - i.E. Invalid routing/ airport/ departure time/ etc/ then the psgr must pay the difference in fares plus the change fee of usd 100.00. Unless restricted by the fare charged - when ticketed from/to chi/hou/nyc/vas - the psgr may check-in end standby from/to one of the airports listed below.
      City - airport
      chi - mdw/ord
      hou - hou/iah
      was - ewe/jfk/lga
