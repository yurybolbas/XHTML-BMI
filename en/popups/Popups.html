Passengers sitting in an exit seat must be able to meet the following criteria and perform the following duties:

  • Have sufficient mobility, strength, or dexterity in both arms, hands, and legs to perform such duties as removing the door, remove obstructions and help other passengers exit the aircraft.
  • Be 15 years of age or older and have the capacity to perform the listed duties without assistance of an adult companion, parent, or other relative.

  • Be able to hear well enough to understand instructions shouted by crewmembers without assistance other than a hearing aid.

  • Be free from the responsibility of caring for someone who cannot be left unattended for the duration of the flight.

Hotel Information

The Rainbow Tower is conveniently located right on Waikiki Beach, and only steps away from our Super Pool. You'll also enjoy the convenience of having three of our premier restaurants located in the Rainbow Tower. This tower offers majestic views of Waikiki Beach, Diamond Head Crater and the Honolulu coastline.

Distinctive Hawaiian Landmark

Rainbow Tower is the signature tower of our historic Resort, recognized by many around the world as an Oahu landmarks. The 31-story tower, framed on either side by mosaic tile rainbows, is listed in the Guinness Book of World Records for displaying two of the world's tallest mosaics.

Distinctive Room Décor

While the Rainbow Tower offers impressive vistas, you will also enjoy comfortable, spacious guest rooms and a wide range of suites, which feature tropical décor. Each room is well-appointed with marble-top desks, wooden armories with intricately carved leaf motifs and colorful tropical bedspreads. Spacious bathrooms with marble countertops complete these rooms.

Home to the Village's Premier Restaurants

Sitting directly on Waikiki Beach, Rainbow Tower is home to two of Oahu's finest dining experiences, Bali by the Sea® and Golden Dragon®. Both fine dining restaurants provide an elegant, seaside atmosphere and have garnered numerous prestigious awards. Also located in the Rainbow Tower is the popular Rainbow Lanai, offering casual, all-day, open-air dining, a relaxed atmosphere and great views of our beautiful beach.
